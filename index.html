How It Works

A simple 4-step process to transform your fitness journey

1

Initial Consultation

We start with a comprehensive questionnaire and video call to understand your goals, limitations, and lifestyle. This helps me create a program that fits you perfectly.

2

Personalized Strategy

Based on our consultation, I design a customized training and nutrition plan tailored to your specific needs, preferences, and schedule.

3

Weekly Video Sessions

We'll meet regularly via video call for personalized coaching, form checks, and program adjustments. These sessions keep you accountable and progressing.

4

Ongoing Support

Between sessions, you'll have access to me for questions, form checks, and motivation. Your program evolves as you progress toward your goals.
